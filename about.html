Robotics
In robotics competitions that I have taken part in there are 3 key parts:
  1. Building and programming a lego robot to complete challenges on a mat
  2. Creating a presentation on an innovative solution to problem posed each year
  3. Another presentation explaining our robot design choices to a panel of judges

Throughout my years of robotics I have taken a role as the main programmer of the robot and also led the writing and research on both presentations.

My work in the team allowed us to win a number of awards over the years:
  • 2018:
    • Tomorrow's Engineers Robotics Challenge UK Champions
  • 2019:
    • First Lego League (FLL) UK Finalists
    • Network Rail Innovation Award Winners
  • 2020:
    • FLL Scottish Final Robot Performance and Robot Knockout Winners
    • FLL Scottish Final Second Overall
    • FLL International Final Qualifiers (cancelled due to covid :( )
  • 2021:
    • FLL UK Final Robot Performance, Robot Knockout and Overall Winners
    • FLL World Final Breakthrough Award Winners
  • 2022:
    • FLL Scottish Final Robot Performance, Robot Knockout and Overall Winners
    • FLL World Final Third Place Robot Design
